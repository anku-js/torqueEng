About Us > Company History

 

     Four Cadets joined the Marine Engineering College (Calcutta) in 1979.  Four years later, they graduated (Class of 1983) and commenced work as marine engineers.  After acquiring various professional Certificates of Competency, they teamed up once again in 1990, to start a partnership Company.  The intention at the time of forming the Company was to render professional services to fill a much felt need in the maritime industry for hydraulic and pneumatic repairs.  Since the promoters are marine engineers who have actually served hands-on in the Indian and international maritime industry, they have a real understanding of customer’s needs.  Until then, a great deal of work was carried by OEM service personnel who were flown into India – a cost prohibitive procedure for most customers in India.  In time to come, the Company was incorporated in 1994, with a paid up capital of Rupees One Hundred Thousand.  

    The core competence in Ship repairs, including structural work gave rise to a diversification in two directions.  The hydraulic and pneumatic applications led to encompassing repairs and servicing of Automation systems.  The exposure to structural repairs led to applications in the floriculture industry – primarily green house construction.  This activity has been expanded independently and C-MET constructed green houses in an exclusive arrangement with Richel Serres de France.
