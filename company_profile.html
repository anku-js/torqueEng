About Us > Company Profile

Established in 1990, Torque has become one of the most widely respected hydraulic ship repair company in India. The company has its office in Navi Mumbai and its workshop in Raigad, Maharashtra.

In addition to our highly skilled Engineer & Technicians who will perform all service and repair work promptly, our dedicated Marine/ Industrial service team also includes knowledgeable sales and parts professionals and engineering personal who genuinely care about serving our client's needs. Our engine service professionals are ready to assist you, whether you need routine or emergency service.

We will be happy to answer your questions or provide additional information about our menu of marine engine services. D-Stroke Engineering is committed to being a one-stop marine & Industrial service source to all over the world. We also provide sailing/riding team worldwide on board to carry out repair works, to assist OEM representative/ Ship staff during major overhauling & retrofitting.

If you on the look out for a repairer who dares time, shrinks cost and exemplifies quality, then Torque is your only answer.
