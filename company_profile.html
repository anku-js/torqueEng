About Us > Company Profile

      

      E stablished in 1990, C-MET  has become one of the most widely respected hydraulic ship repair company in India.    The company has  its office in Ballard Estate , Mumbai and its workshop in Rabale, New Bombay .

    Today C-MET has a core team of 8 engineers, 15 skilled technicians and supervisors and 25 fitters with more than 15 years experience.  The  company is managed by four marine engineers with a passion for setting benchmarks and incorporating the latest  technology in ship repairs. We are a team of Senior Marine Engineers with a collective experience of more than  100 years at sea and ashore. From this background we are able to draw on our combined knowledge and contacts throughout the industry for efficient and cost-effective responses to your enquiries.

Surging Ahead with Foresight C-MET, since its inception, has been setting up new horizons in building the best infrastructure for hydraulic repairs in India !
